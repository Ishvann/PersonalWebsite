About

I'm currently a grad student at Stanford studying CS. I went to UT Austin for my undergrad and graduated in 2014. I'll be interning at Facebook in 2015.

I'm a research assistant for the Computer Vision Lab (SAIL). My team focuses on AI-Assisted Healthcare. I'm also a TA for CS 108 (Object Oriented Programming) during Autumn and Winter quarters. A summary of my technical interests and projects are below:
